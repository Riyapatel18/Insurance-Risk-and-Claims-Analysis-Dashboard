# Insurance Risk and Claims Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The Insurance Risk and Claims Analysis Dashboard is a Business Intelligence project developed using Microsoft Excel and Power BI. The project focuses on analyzing insurance claim data to identify high-risk customer segments, vehicle-related claim trends, demographic patterns, and coverage zone insights.

The dashboard helps insurance companies make data-driven decisions related to pricing strategies, risk management, customer targeting, and profitability improvement.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze insurance claims data
- Identify high-risk factors affecting claim amounts
- Understand demographic and vehicle usage patterns
- Provide actionable business recommendations
- Build an interactive dashboard for decision-making

## 📊 Key Dashboard Metrics
- Total Policies: 38K
- Total Claims: $187.82M
- Average Claim Frequency: 0.51
- Average Claim Amount: $5K

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
### Vehicle Usage Analysis
- Private vehicles contribute to over 80% of total claim amounts
- Commercial vehicles account for approximately 20%

### High-Risk Age Group
- Customers aged 36–45 generate the highest claim amounts

### Vehicle Make Analysis
- Ford and Chevrolet vehicles have the highest total claims

### Geographic Distribution
- Claims are evenly distributed across Urban, Rural, and Suburban zones

### Education & Marital Status
- Bachelor's degree holders show the highest claim amounts
- Single customers contribute significantly higher claims
